This is a converter dedicated to the CSV. It started as simply a CSV to JSON converter, but recently I added the ability to go back again, and added a JSON to CSV converter.

CSV, to {"JSON"} {"JSON"} to CSV

As a programmer, my goal is to convert as many Excel and CSV files as I can to JSON, but I can understand that there are many data stewards out there who would rather have their data as CSV and Excel or Google Doc files.
